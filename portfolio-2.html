About Me

I'm an engineering student who completed two years of intensive CPGE preparation at Salman Al-Farisi, Salé — one of Morocco's most rigorous academic tracks, covering applied mathematics, mechanics, electromagnetism, and computer science.

My work lives at the intersection of embedded systems, software, and visual design. I like understanding things from first principles — whether that's a PID controller stabilizing a drone or a layout that makes information feel effortless to navigate.

Outside of engineering, I work with Photoshop, video editing, and web technologies regularly. I believe good engineers communicate their work visually just as well as technically.
